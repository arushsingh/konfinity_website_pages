Our Pedagogy

A mixture of science and philosophy, we follow Harkness method of education in which students follow socratic questioning approach to effectively collaborate and discuss meaningful ways to analyze, synthesize and present their learning. Students progress by working on full stack team projects to solve challenges and acquire a deeper knowledge through our platform.
